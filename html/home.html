Our Mission Statement

Women in Computing seeks to unite the CS majors at Valdosta State University while encouraging and celebrating gender diversity in Computer Science and other STEM majors. We seek to empower and mentor women at Valdosta State University who are interested in technical fields.


Amanda Mitchell, 2017
