About Us


"Science is not a boy's game, it's not a girl's game. It's everyone's game. It's about where we are and where we're going." - Nichelle Nichols


Women in Computer Science at Valdosta State University is a group created to encourage women in technology to strive for their best. We plan to have community outreach programs to get young women excited about the world of computing. We also would love to have the opportunity to have workshops, work with other schools in the area, and have competitions to put our skills to the test. Our mission is to provide a positive and empowering environment for all women in STEM majors on campus. We also encourage professors and men to join us on projects and outreach to help our group grow and thrive on Valdosta State’s campus.
